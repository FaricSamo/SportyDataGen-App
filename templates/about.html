Sport science research, artificial intelligence methods and other computational methods are in a huge expansion. Broad specter of analyzers is being developed and new techniques of discovering knowledge from sport’s data are proposed. The last listed feature however presents a great bottleneck of sport’s data analysts. In order to prevent difficult process of searching the data, and support the analyst’s opinion, we propose a sophisticated preprocessing techniques, named SportyDataGen. Its main mission “to support research process”, is packed into a freely available online generator of sport training data. Main features of SportyDataGen are:

Easy to use

SportyDataGen is used with much ease. User may select interested parameters and sport’s data is generated. To use SportyDataGen, no annoying register/login features are needed and no irritating advertisements are present.

Real data

SportyDataGen generates data obtained from different athletes. Amateur and professional athletes are taken into account. SportyDataGen totally relies on references and therefore does not offer randomly generated data.

Easy sharing of generated datasets

Generated datasets are downloaded from a server. Each of them obtains unique dataset link for the purposes of citing in a publication or sharing to your friends.

Easy reuse and replication

In any case, reviewers may be equipped by a full dataset and to convince themselves by reproducing your research.

Multiple format output

A strive to help researchers with time consuming tasks is taken into account. Sport’s data can therefore be downloaded in a multiple formats, including .csv and .json files.

Permanent storage

Generated datasets are permanently stored on a server and daily backups are conducted.

Rising data!

Data is added into our database regularly. To have an access for sport volunteers for donating sports’s data is in the accordance with the mission of SportyDataGen.

Support for multiple sports

Current version of SportyDataGen supports biking and running. The SportyDataGen team is happy to announce that support for more sports, including swimming and multisport is planned.

Under development

Current version of SportyDataGen is not the end. New ideas will be implemented in the coming versions of SportyDataGen.
